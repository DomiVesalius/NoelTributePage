Youtuber, Comedian, Actor, Writer, Producer, Web Developer

Noel Miller is a Canadian comedian that first had his start on Vine with his famous "Skinny Penis" meme video. Now, he has expanded into several other areas of internet culture such as music, YouTube, podcasting, and Twitch streaming.

Tiny Meat Gang

Tiny Meat Gang (TMG) is the name of the duo of Noel Miller and his friend Cody Ko. Together they create weekly comedy podcasts and occasionally release music which had started as a joke but eventually grew into a semi-serious artist duo as they have collaborated with American artist Blackbear. Some of their most popular songs include:

  • NOEL - LOOPHOLE
  • TMG - Walk Man
  • TMG - Broke Bitch
  • TMG - No Flex
  • TMG - Sofia
  • Blackbear & TMG - Short Kings Anthem
